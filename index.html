Welcome to Virtual

Bubble Blower!

 

Click on the screen to

start.

 

Button instructions:

 

B - Click screen to blow a

bubble. Hold mouse

button down for larger

bubbles. Don't make them

too large, or they'll pop!

 

C - Click bubble to give it

a random color.

 

F - Click bubble to freeze

or unfreeze it.

 

P - Click bubble to pop it.
